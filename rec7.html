5 Crispy Stirfried Water Spinach with Chicken Liver Recipe

A healhty meal that you will enjoy with your family

TOTAL TIME:
Prep/Total Time: 30 min.

Ingredients
  • 250 g of fresh water spinach
  • 150 g chicken liver
  • 5 cloves of garlic, coarsely minced
  • 1 head onion, coarsely chopped
  • 2 tbsp of oil
Sauce:
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup water

Directions
  1. Heat oil and saut√© garlic until slightly brown.
  2. Stir fry onions until fragrant.
  3. Add the chicken liver and stir fry until tender.
  4. Throw in the water spinach stalks for a minute then add the leaves.
  5. Pour in the sauce mixture and toss to blend.
  6. Stir fry very quickly or until the leaves wilt and the stalks are still tender crisp.
  7. Do not overcook to preserve the crispiness of stalks and the bright-green leaves.
  8. Serve immediately with hot rice.
